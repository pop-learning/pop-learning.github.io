Design And Build For HTML Email

About

What is the purpose of this course?

This course shows you how to design and build for HTML email in accordance with best-practice guidelines. The modules cover four key areas: email rendering issues, email design guidelines, email content management, and email testing.

Who is the target audience?

This course was created for new starts to the Poppulo Design and Support Team, as well any other employees who may interact with customers and therefore wish to have a stronger understanding of HTML email - one of Poppulo's core products. As a new start to the Design or Support team, you likely have an introductory knowledge of HTML and CSS, but you are not yet expected to be familiar with the differences between web and email design.

How should I progress through the course?

From the 'Home' page you can choose a module. Once you complete the course, you can use it as a future resource by revisiting desired content. Access the 'Course Map' page to see all of the course modules, units, and lessons.

Will there any assessment throughout the course?

At the end of each unit there will be a quiz or assessment. Each quiz involves answering a series of questions while assessments provide you with tasks or assignments to complete. These assessments will be reviewed by the Design team lead, who will provide you with feedback.
