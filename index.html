Introduction

The purpose of this course is to show you how to design and build for HTML email in accordance with best-practice guidelines. To enable you to communicate effectively with customers on the subject of email, and to build and test email templates.

The modules cover four key areas: email rendering issues, email design guidelines, email content management, and email testing. It is recommended that you progress through the modules sequentially, beginning with module one. On completion of the course, you can use the Course Map page to access individual lessons.
