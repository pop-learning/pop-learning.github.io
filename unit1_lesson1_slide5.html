HTML Email Restrictions

iFrames

iFrames (inline frames) can contain scripts or link to malicious content. Most email clients will block them for security purposes.

Only a handful of email clients support iFrames, and they are best avoided in HTML email.

In the iFrame on the right, you can see which email clients support iFrames.
