HTML Email Restrictions

Custom Fonts

Email clients will not display a font unless it is installed on the recipient's machine. A handful of email clients support web fonts using @font-face and @import, but best practice recommends the use of web-safe fonts to ensure a consistent appearance for your email.

Web-safe fonts are fonts that are installed on all computers as standard. Some examples of web-safe fonts would be Arial, Verdana, Georgia, and Tahoma.
