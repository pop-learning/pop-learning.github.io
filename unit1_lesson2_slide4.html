Coding for HTML Email

Do not rely on images to communicate your message

Email clients can block images until the reader enables them. For best practice use at least an equal balance of images and live text. Consider whether images are necessary for your message and ensure that the key message of the email is communicated through text rather than images.

The email on the right of the screen relies heavily on images. It is essential to use alt (alternative) text that will display when images are blocked. Alt text is used to provide descriptive information about the images.

In this example when the navigation link images are blocked, alt text replaces them. However, the main image lacks information, and the alt text is vague and non-descriptive.
