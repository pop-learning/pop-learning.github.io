Email Clients and Market Share

Email opens by platform

In the last number of years, we've seen a dramatic increase in mobile opens. Mobile now accounts for 54% of all email opens. The majority of mobile market share belongs to iOS (Apple) devices. This is good news as iOS mail apps have excellent support for HTML and CSS.

Native vs Downloaded mobile apps

What is meant by a 'native' mail app?

'Native' simply refers to the mail app that was preinstalled on the phone. You can also download non-native mail apps from the App store, e.g. Gmail for mobile.
