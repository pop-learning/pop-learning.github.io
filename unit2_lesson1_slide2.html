Outlook rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Outlook ignores margins and padding applied to images


Solution A:Use hspace or vspace. Hspace applies horizontal space to an image, and vspace applies vertical space to an image.

<img src="example.jpg" align="right" vspace="25" hspace="25" />

Solution B:Wrap a table around the image and apply the padding to the <td> cell.

<table width="100%" border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0">
<tr>
<td class="imageCell" style="padding-left: 25px; padding-bottom: 25px;">
<img src="example.jpg" align="right" />
</td>
</tr>
</table>

Solution C:Include the padding as part of the image itself.


Image inside a table with padding (blue)
applied to the <td> cell.
