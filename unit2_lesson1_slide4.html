Outlook rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Outlook 2007 and 2010 page-break bug


Solution:Editing or rearranging of email content.

This is a bug related to the email client, not the Poppulo template. There isn't a direct fix, but there is a workaround. Often the easiest solution is to re-arrange or edit your content.

If the break is happening in the middle of a paragraph, try shortening the paragraph or reducing the size of an image above or below the break. This can cause the break to fall between articles instead of in the middle of them, reducing the unwanted spacing or removing it entirely.

Key points:
  • The page-break bug is caused by the email client, not an error in the email code.
  • The customer is responsible for managing the appearance of the page-break bug by editing or rearranging their email content.
