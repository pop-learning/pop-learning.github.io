Outlook rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Long images clipped in Outlook

Outlook cuts images off that exceed the maximum height of 1728px. This image height limit exists for Outlook 2007 and 2010, which will clip the top of overly long images. This is another example of a display issue caused by Outlook's Microsoft Word rendering engine.

Solution A: Resize your image to 1700px high or less.

Solution B: Divide the long image into slices using Fireworks or
Photoshop. Add the slices into the email as individual
images. Remove any spacing or padding between the
slices to give the appearance of one long image.
