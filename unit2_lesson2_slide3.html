Gmail rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Gmail converts numbers and URLs to links.


This means that numbers or URLs that you do not want to hyperlink will still have the appearance of an active link.

For further information please contact 021 123 4567 or email info@address.com

Solution: Insert an anchor <a> tag around the URL and format it to match the rest of the non-linked text. Do not add a href URL address. Instead apply a class name and target this class to apply your preferred styles.


For further information please contact
<a class="nonLinkedStyle">021 123 4567</a> or email
<a class="nonLinkedStyle">info@address.com</a>

Normally you need to inline your CSS, but the Poppulo platform will automatically inline all the CSS from the external stylesheet.
