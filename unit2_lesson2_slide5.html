Gmail rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Gmail displays a download icon over large images

Gmail places a download icon over large unlinked images. This is undesirable behaviour as it offers unintended click targets to the email recipient and it deviates from the intended design of the email.



Solution: Display of the download icon can be prevented by ensuring that images larger than 300 x 150 pixels are wrapped with a link.
