Lotus Notes rendering issues and solutions

Lotus Notes is used primarily in internal communications. This email client was well known for its serious rendering issues, but has made substantial improvements in it's newest version, IBM Notes 9. The different versions of Lotus Notes can be divided into three categories of HTML and CSS support:

Poor support Improved support but still limited Good support

Problem: Border styles overwritten

Any borders in your email will default to thick black border in Lotus Notes.

Solution: Avoid using border styles in emails that will be viewed in earlier versions of Lotus Notes.
