Lotus Notes rendering issues and solutions

Problem: Padding is not supported

There is limited support for the padding attribute in early versions of Lotus Notes.

Solution: Spacing can be created using tables, or where images are supported (Version 7 onwards) you can use spacer GIFs.

Lack of support for background-color and PNG images.

Colour and images can be fundamental in creating eye-catching emails. For older versions of Lotus Notes (6.5 and 7), the email design will have to be more minimalistic and simple in its approach. It's crucial to manage the customer's expectation of what can be achieved. The CSS attribute 'background-color' is not well supported and Lotus Notes 6.5 may not always display images, meaning that the email message must be communicated solely through text.
