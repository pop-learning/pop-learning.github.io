Lotus Notes rendering issues and solutions

Testing emails in Lotus Notes

Do not rely solely on an email testing platform for testing Lotus Notes. The most reliable testing method is to send the test email directly to the customer's email and request screenshots. Once you learn that a customer is using Lotus Notes, send a test sample email to determine the level of HTML and CSS support available. This will help to ensure that your email design is appropriate for the customer's version of Lotus Notes.

Customers may state that they are using a newer version of Lotus Notes, but if an older version was installed previously then it may impact the render of the email.
