Unit 3: Assessment

Present a topic for a design discussion


In your role as a Designer you are expected to keep up to date with current design trends. Every two weeks the design team hold 15 minute design discussions. Design can often be a very subjective topic, and these sessions are a valuable opportunity to gather insights and opinions from your colleagues.

Choose one of the following topics to research and write a 400 word article about your findings. You are encouraged to express your opinion on the topic, and explain the reasoning behind your thinking.
  • White space
  • Above the fold
  • Minimalist design trend
You will then present your article in a design discussion and ask your colleagues for their thoughts.

These design discussions help to simulate the type of conversations you may have with a customer when presenting a template design. It's vital to develop both your verbal and written communication skills.
