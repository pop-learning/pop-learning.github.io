Email Layout Design

Design around your content


Are you creating a design for an existing communication? Ask the customer to share an example of a previous email with you. This will give you an indication of the type and amount of content that will be added to each email. You can create a visually pleasing design, but it also needs to cater for the customer's specific content.
  • Will video and images feature heavily or is the content primarily text?
  • How easily can the customer source images?
  • Do they have an in-house design team?
  • How many articles will they typically have per email?
These are all factors to consider. For example, if the customer's content is primarily text then don't provide the customer with a design where images are the focal point of the email.
