Email Layout Design

Scannability and Attention Span


It's important to design emails that can be easily scanned by the reader. They must be able to quickly assess the email content and then focus on material specific to their interests. Studies by the Nielsen Norman group show that email recipients will not read all of the content.

Litmus reported that in 2016, recipients spent an average of 11.1 seconds reading an email. This highlights how important it is to present information in a clear and concise manner.
