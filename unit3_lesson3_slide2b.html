Lesson 3: Design Call Communications

Common Customer Questions

Select an appropriate response to the customer's question. This is not a quiz,
and you can click on each potential answer to see all possible feedback.

A. We will create up to two proposed designs, and you can request multiple small amends to a proposed design. We will make up to three rounds of these small changes.
B. You can request as many revisions as needed until you're happy with the final design.
C. We create no more than two proposed designs, and you can only request small amendments. You're limited to three rounds of requested small changes.
This is the correct response. You have been clear about the number of designs you are willing to create, and the amount of revisions you will make to these. You have explained the limitations without using negative language.
This answer is accurate, but uses more negative language such as 'no more', 'only', and 'limited'. Answer A phrases the information in a more positive manner.
This answer may leave you obligated to make extensive and ongoing design changes. You need to be clear about the number of proposed designs you will create and how many revisions you will make to them.
