B. You can request minor changes after the template is built. An example of a minor amendment is colour changes. As a general guideline, any larger changes that require more than 30 minutes to complete will be billed for.
This answer is correct. It reassures the customer that small changes can still be made once the template is built, while specifying that anything other than minor amends will be charged for.
