Common Customer Questions

Select an appropriate response to the customer's question. This is not a quiz,
and you can click on each potential answer to see all possible feedback.

A. Once a template is approved, it must be built and then tested. The turnaround time of five to seven working days was clearly stated at the beginning of the design process.
B. Once a template is approved, it must be built and then tested. This process takes five to seven working days to complete. It is essential to complete thorough testing across all email clients to ensure that your template is free of rendering issues.
C. Once a template is approved, it must be built and then tested. We'll do our best to deliver the template as quickly as possible.
This answer is correct. It clearly states the turnaround time required to build and test the template, and explains why this turnaround time is needed.
This answer is accurate, but unnecessarily confrontational as a first response.

If a customer responds negatively to the stated turnaround times and your justification for them, then it is acceptable to politely remind them that these timelines were stated at the very beginning of the design process.
This answer is not appropriate. It has an apologetic tone, and suggests that the customer was not provided with all timelines at the beginning of the design process.

By stating that you will build the template "as quickly as possible", you encourage frequent emails from the customer regarding your progress. Be very clear about your expected turnaround time.
