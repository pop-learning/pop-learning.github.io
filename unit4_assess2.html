Unit 4: Assessment

Redesign an email layout as a mobile-first design


Your task is to take a standard desktop email design and redesign it for mobile first use. Keep the same article content (text and images) in the new mobile-first design. Redesign the email according to the best-practice guidelines for mobile-first design.

Once you have created your proposed template design, submit it to your team lead for review and feedback. Your team lead will respond to you with any helpful feedback they can provide; they will discuss what you have done well and any points of the design that you need to strengthen.

Guidelines
  • Keep the same article content (text and images) in the new mobile-first design.
  • Adjust the template width.
  • Adjust the template layout.
