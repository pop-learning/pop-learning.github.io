Responsive Mobile Design

What is Responsive Design?

Responsive design uses CSS3 media queries to adapt the layout of an email or webpage according to the screen size it is viewed on. This allows you to create an optimal viewing experience for the different screen sizes of tablets and mobile phones.

The goal of responsive design is to encourage readers to engage with the email by presenting a layout that intuitive to use and easy to read. Pinching the screen, side scrolling, or trying to view a scaled-down desktop view can result in the reader abandoning the email.

On the right on the screen you will see a wireframe example of how a responsive design adapts to different platforms. Notice how the layout adapts and the content areas stack on smaller screen sizes. Any images will also scale to fit the screen size.
