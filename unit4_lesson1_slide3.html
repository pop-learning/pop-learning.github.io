Responsive Mobile Design

Why use Responsive Design?

Email recipients need a positive user experience on both mobile and desktop. Although conversion rates are reportedly higher when emails are opened on a desktop platform, mobile now accounts for 54% of all email opens, so ensuring a strong responsive design is vital.

Email recipients have a low tolerance for non-responsive email, with 80% of survey respondents stating that they would delete an email that did not display well on mobile.

There are a number of uses for responsive design, including:
  • Changing font sizes
  • Changing the hierachy of content
  • Adjustments to spacing
  • Scaling of images
  • Changing of navigation
