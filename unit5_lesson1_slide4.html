Email Content Management

Appropriate tone of voice

Written communication can be prone to misinterpretation. Without visual cues and vocal tones extra care must be taken to ensure your message is received as intended.

The same message may be interpreted differently by different people. Clarity is key.


The tone of voice should be appropriate for your audience. Many internal communicators now gravitate toward a more approachable tone, but exercise caution if introducing any humour into your email as readers may take your words literally.

You should consider the difference between the psychological state of the writer and the recipient. If the recipient is particularly stressed or under pressure they may interpret the message with a negative bias.
