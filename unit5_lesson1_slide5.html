Email Content Management

Encourage two-way communication

Video is a popular way creating a sense of face-to-face communication, with organizational Leaders frequently speaking to their teams via video messages. Asking readers for comments and questions is essential for an engaged audience. Poppulo's whitepaper on 'Communicating effectively in the workforce – reaching beyond HQ' notes that "when people weigh-in, they buy-in".

Surveys in email act as a feedback loop; allowing readers to contribute content for future emails, ask questions relevant to the current email content, and provide immediate feedback. Poppulo's commenting functionality facilitates open discussion between employees, with the ability to submit and publish comments to the email article page, where they are visible to all readers.
