Email Subject Lines

How To Choose a Subject Line

It is common practice to write your email subject line after you have written your email content. The subject line should be guided by the content. Above all your subject should be honest and accurate as it creates an expectation of the content. Avoid subject lines that are vague or misleading. Click-bait subject lines are frustrating for the reader, and make them less likely to engage with your future emails.

Ask yourself what's in it for the customer? What can they gain by opening this email? You should only send the email if there is something of value to offer.

How Long Should Your Subject Line Be?

Desktop email clients can display approximately 60 characters in a subject line. However, you also need to remember that the same email displayed on mobile may only display 25 to 30 characters of the subject line. The message of the subject line shouldn't be lost or skewed on mobile.
