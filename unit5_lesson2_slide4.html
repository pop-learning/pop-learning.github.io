Email Subject Lines

Subject Line Keywords

Return Path compiled a report on email subject lines. One particular area of the report looked at effectiveness of different keywords.

Overall, "Urgency" subject lines performed the best, followed by "Benefit" and "Command" subject lines. Keywords commonly found in "Clickbait" headlines were far less successful in email subject lines.
