Sending a test email

Send a test email from the Poppulo platform to Email on Acid

All Poppulo email templates are built from seed templates within the Poppulo platform. Once a template is built it must be sent to an email testing platform for review and approval.

In this lesson you will view a step-by-step guide to sending a test email from the Poppulo platform to the Email on Acid platform.
